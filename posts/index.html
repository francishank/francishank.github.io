Types of Coffee beans

Coffee Beans Coffee beans are the seeds of the coffee plant, known scientifically as Coffea. They are harvested from the plant’s berries or cherries when they reach maturity. Types of Coffee Beans Type Origin Flavor Profile Caffeine Content Popular Varieties Arabica Ethiopia, Colombia, Brazil Smooth, acidic, with hints of fruit, floral, and sugary notes Moderate to low Bourbon, Typica, Caturra, Catuai, SL28, Geisha Robusta Central and Western Africa Strong, harsh, with earthy and nutty undertones High Nganda, Java, Conilon, Ugandan Bugisu, Kaapi Royale Processing Methods Coffee beans undergo various processing methods after harvesting, which significantly influence their flavor and aroma.
