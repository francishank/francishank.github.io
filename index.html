Types of Coffee beans

Coffee Beans Coffee beans are the seeds of the coffee plant, known scientifically as Coffea. They are harvested from the plant’s berries or cherries when they reach maturity. Types of Coffee Beans 1. Arabica Origin: Arabica beans are believed to have originated in Ethiopia. Flavor Profile: Arabica beans tend to have a smoother, sweeter flavor with hints of fruit and sugar. Caffeine Content: Arabica beans generally contain less caffeine than Robusta beans.
